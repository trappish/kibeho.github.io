Cultivating Excellence and Inclusivity at GS Mère du Verbe Kibeho.

GS Mère du Verbe Kibeho is a vibrant community of learners, encompassing students, parents, and staff, all committed to fostering a learning environment that is both academically challenging and culturally inclusive. Together, we strive to achieve excellence in academics while making a positive impact on our community and beyond. At GS Mère du Verbe Kibeho, we are dedicated to not only reaching academic success but also shaping the future through our shared efforts and values.
